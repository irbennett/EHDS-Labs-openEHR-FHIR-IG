# openEHR Templates

openEHR templates constrain archetypes to define the specific data sets used in this Implementation Guide.
Templates are developed in the openEHR Clinical Knowledge Manager (CKM) or compatible tools and are
serialised as Operational Templates (`.opt` files) for deployment to an openEHR CDR.

are included in `input/includes/openehr-input/templates/`.
